Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Based on the data obtained from the API call, there is no particular advantage or disadvantage from one latitude to another in regards to cloudiness. There is some stratification, perhaps due to rounding the results from data-gathering, and, it could be argued, slightly more cloudiness in the more northern climes.
