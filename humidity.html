Latitude vs. Humidity

As may be seen in this plot, on the sampled date, humidity was notably higher in the northern latitudes than in the southern hemishphere. This may still be determined despite the presence of one essentially-impossible outlier -- 290% humidity (!) -- in the data obtained from the API.
