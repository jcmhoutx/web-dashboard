The Effects of Latitude on Weather

To gain insight regarding the effects of geographical latitude on weather conditions, I performed an analysis on weather conditions relative to latitude. First, a random series of latitude and longitude values were generated, then those values were used to determine the nearest city to those coordinates via a Python library, citypy. With the list of identified cities, I was able to return the current weather conditions for those locations over a wide range of latitudes by leveraging the API at openweathermap.org.

This site contains plots (created using Matplotlib) to visualize the results of my analysis, as well as information regarding the underlying data. While the results may help to provide some insights about weather variance with latitude, several caveats apply. Most notably, this data is only from one date. Not only does this indicate that this particular day could be an outlier, but results could vary widely over the course of the year, as seasons change, as well as from year to year.
